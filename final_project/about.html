About Us

We are fortunate to receive tremendous support from our community, which plays a crucial role in advancing our mission.

Driven by our commitment to faith, education, and community, we are actively working to meet the needs of our growing congregation. While our current facility is cherished, it is limited in both space and functionality. To realize our vision, we are raising funds to support the development of our new center. Together, we are dedicated to making this vision a reality.

Our mission is to build a mosque, school, and community center that will serve as a beacon of faith and learning.
